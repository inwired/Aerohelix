AER HELIX

A hybrid Savonius–Darrieus helical vertical-axis wind turbine, designed for low-speed coastal environments in Egypt.

🏆

Achievement

Exceeded design requirements: 594J output (vs 150J target), 18% enhancement (vs 10% required), stable at 6 m/s wind.
